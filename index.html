The concept

A flexible place for different needs throughout the day.

During the day, Fiore offers a calm and welcoming café environment for studying, working, relaxing or meeting friends. Guests can also create their own flower bouquets while drinking coffee.

The flowers are both decoration and product, which makes the space feel natural and constantly changing. Local artists can use Fiore as a showroom, while workshops encourage guests to learn something new and socialise. In the evening, the atmosphere smoothly turns into a relaxed bar with beer, wine and long drinks.

Community-based

Our vision

Not built around aesthetics alone.

Fiore is not about paying more for an Instagrammable café. The concept focuses on creating a place people return to regularly because they feel welcome, can stay longer and connect with others. By combining affordability, creativity and a smooth day-to-night transition, Fiore offers a modern third place for city residents, students and young adults.
