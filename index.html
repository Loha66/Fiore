The concept

An open space that changes with the day.

Fiore combines a café, flower experience, local showroom and evening bar in one flexible location. The goal is not to create another expensive and highly aesthetic café, but a welcoming place where different people feel comfortable for different reasons.

Flowers are part of the atmosphere and can also be purchased or arranged by guests themselves. This makes the interior dynamic, creative and connected to the people using the space.

The experience

From studying to socialising — without changing places.

Fiore is made for longer stays, spontaneous meetings and small moments of creativity. In exam time, students can use the space to study. On other days, guests may come for coffee, a workshop, flowers or a relaxed drink in the evening.
