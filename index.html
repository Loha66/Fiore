Coffee. Flowers. Community.

A welcoming café that blooms into a relaxed bar.

Fiore is an affordable and inclusive third place in the city. During the day, it offers coffee, studying, flower creation and local art. In the evening, it smoothly turns into a relaxed bar for meeting friends.

Book a first visit

The idea

More than just another café.

Fiore combines different needs in one open space. Guests can study, work, meet friends, arrange their own flower bouquets or take part in creative workshops. The flowers are part of the decoration, but they can also be bought, which makes the space feel natural and constantly changing.

In the evening, the atmosphere changes smoothly through lighting, music and drinks. Instead of becoming a loud party bar, Fiore stays relaxed and social, offering beer, wine and long drinks.

Why Fiore?

Built around inclusivity, affordability and creativity.

Inclusive

Fiore is designed as a welcoming place for different people, budgets and reasons to visit.

Affordable

Fair prices and student-friendly offers, including possible coffee flats during exam periods.

Creative

Guests can create their own bouquets, join workshops and discover local artists.

Sustainable

Plant-based milk is cheaper than cow milk to encourage sustainable choices in an easy way.

“Fiore is not about paying more for aesthetics. It is about creating a place where people feel welcome, stay longer and connect with others.”
