The Concept

An open place that changes with the day.

Fiore is an affordable and inclusive café that offers a warm place for studying, working, relaxing or meeting friends. Unlike many highly aesthetic and expensive cafés, Fiore focuses on creating a space where people feel welcome and do not feel pressured to leave quickly.

During the day, guests can create their own flower bouquets while having coffee. The flowers are not only products, but also part of the decoration, which makes the interior feel natural and constantly changing. Local artists can use Fiore as a showroom, while workshops encourage guests to learn something new and socialise.

In the evening, the atmosphere changes smoothly into a relaxed bar with beer, wine and long drinks. Fiore is therefore not just a café, but a flexible third place for different needs throughout the day.

Our Vision

Not just another café.

Fiore wants to be a place where people feel at home. It combines affordability, creativity and community in a way that makes the space accessible for students, young adults, city residents and local stakeholders.

The concept is not about paying more for aesthetics. It is about creating a place where people stay longer, connect with others and belong.

What makes Fiore different?

  • Inclusive atmosphere instead of exclusivity
  • Flowers as decoration and product
  • Workshops and local art as community elements
  • Smooth transition from café to evening bar
  • Plant-based milk cheaper than cow milk
